About Jordan McLamb Music

Jordan enjoys working with couples to make the music selection process stress-free and meaningful. Whether you seek solo guitar or guitar with vocals, Jordan can provide each with professionalism, a positive attitude and attentive communication. He happily takes requests and is competent with all genres of music.


Services Offered

Couples can request Jordan for their ceremony and cocktail hour. As a part of his services, Jordan includes all the necessary audio equipment for the ceremony such as microphones for the officiant, readers, and a high-quality amplification system. Couples can also hear sample music before booking him.
